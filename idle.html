How this page works

This mode now has its own shell, but it still reuses the current Battle team and the existing Idle routes from your backend.

1

Prepare team

Idle reads the same 6-Pokémon team saved in Play Hub, so you do not need a second team system here.

2

Choose tier

Select Route, Elite, or Legend depending on how hard you want the expedition to scale.

3

Run expedition

The page keeps countdown, progress, backend status refresh, and claim or cancel actions.

4

Claim results

When the run is complete, claim EXP, Pokédollars, and item drops from the existing Idle endpoints.
